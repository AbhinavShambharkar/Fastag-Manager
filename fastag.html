Welcome to MyFastag Manager

NHAI FASTag is a perfect solution for a hassle-free trip on national highways, it enables automatic deduction of toll charges and lets you pass through the toll plaza without stopping for the cash transaction.

FEATURES

Cashless transaction

Customer don't need to worry about carrying cash for the toll payments.

Online Recharge

User can recharge his tag account online through Debit Card or Net Banking

Travel History

Customer can check the travel history and their recent toll payments.

About NHAI...



NHAI FASTag provides facility of online toll collection and recharge to the customer. It helps to provide fast and easier option for toll payments.To encourage the cashless transaction and save the time and fuel spent by customer during toll collection. NHAI is responsible for the development, maintenance and management of National Highways entrusted to it and/or matters connected or incidental thereto. NHAI enters into Concession Agreements for design, construction, operation and maintenance of highways by DBFOT Concessionaires.
